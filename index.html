Lyon, the new French blockchain hub

The blockchain technology is impacting our economy. Many entrepreneurs see an opportunity to create new business models. In consequence, the AMF (Autorité des Marchés Financiers) organizes regular consulting sessions with those new actors to better understand their needs ; so to give the French government the means to adapt and write specific law about the blockchains.

Innovative and entrepreneurial French cities like Paris and Lyon are creating the necessary conditions for blockchain-based projects to emerge and develop. Let's take a closer look at the start-ups ecosystem in Lyon, a dynamic city for economic growth and innovation labelled "French Tech Metropolis".
